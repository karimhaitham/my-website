Event

One of our special activities is the robotics workshop events that established in schools & clubs.We believe that “learn by doing” is the most effective way to achieve our learning objectives! We create a special package in 3~4 hours that includes teaching programming & robotics basics then jumping to apply in an exciting competitions!! (Each individual will participate in all activities)

Courses

Distinctive educational path suitable for the age group 9-16 years and aims to give them programming skills from beginner level to professionalism, using innovative tools and methods to ensure the delivery of scientific content in exciting environment. Curriculums are Prepared by experienced engineers and programmers to cover market needs. Used software platforms are created specially for targeted age by global companies (google, Microsoft,…).

Competition

We participated in many tournaments, giving our students the opportunity to compete, teach and learn from their peers at the level of the country and the world. Competition is a great way for motivation and fast learning. Competitions build important interpersonal skills that prepare students for the workplace later in life. It also helps them succeed socially and academically in school.
